<p><p>Form-In-Place (FIP) Gaskets are used in various industries for creating seals between two surfaces. There are two main types of FIP gaskets: Conductive Form-In-Place Gaskets and Non-Conductive Form-In-Place Gaskets. Conductive FIP gaskets are used in electronic devices where electromagnetic interference must be minimized. Non-Conductive FIP gaskets are used in applications where electrical conductivity is not desired. Both types of FIP gaskets are customizable, easy to apply, and provide effective sealing properties in a wide range of environments.</p></p>
