<p><strong>The Banking CRM Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cloud-based</li><li>On-premises</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Banking CRM software is available in two main market types: Cloud-based and On-premises. Cloud-based CRM software is a subscription-based service that is hosted on the vendor's servers and accessed via the internet, providing flexibility and scalability for banks without the need for extensive IT infrastructure. On-premises CRM software, on the other hand, is installed and managed on the bank's own servers, giving them more control over data security and customization options but requiring higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Both options have their own advantages and considerations for banks to evaluate based on their specific needs and capabilities.</p></p>
