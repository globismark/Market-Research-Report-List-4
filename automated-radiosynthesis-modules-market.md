<p><p>Automated radiosynthesis modules are classified into two main types: fully automated and semi-automated. Fully automated modules perform all steps of the radiotracer synthesis process without any manual intervention, offering high efficiency and reproducibility. On the other hand, semi-automated modules require some manual intervention during certain steps of the process, providing more flexibility to the user. Both types of modules are widely used in nuclear medicine and research laboratories for the synthesis of radiotracers used in positron emission tomography (PET) imaging.</p></p>
