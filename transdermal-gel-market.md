<p><ul><li>Ionophoresis</li><li>Electroporation</li><li>Sonophoresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transdermal gel market consists of various types such as ionophoresis, electroporation, and sonophoresis. Ionophoresis involves the use of ions to carry drugs through the skin, electroporation uses electrical pulses to create temporary pores in the skin for drug delivery, and sonophoresis uses ultrasound to enhance drug penetration. These methods are all non-invasive and provide a convenient way to administer medication without the need for injections or pills, making them popular options in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
