<p><strong>The Programmable Application Specific Integrated Circuit (ASIC) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-custom Design</li><li>Gate-array Based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Programmable ASIC market includes three main types: Full-custom Design, Gate-array Based, and Others. Full-custom Design involves designing every aspect of the circuit for maximum performance and efficiency but with high costs and long lead times. Gate-array Based ASICs use pre-designed logic blocks that can be quickly customized for specific applications, offering a balance between customization and time-to-market. Other types of ASICs may include semi-custom designs or structured ASICs, offering varying degrees of customization and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
