<p><p>Sprayed concrete is a construction method where concrete is applied through a hose and nozzle at high velocity onto a surface. There are two main types of sprayed concrete markets: wet sprayed concrete and dry sprayed concrete. Wet sprayed concrete involves pre-mixing the concrete before it is sprayed, providing a more uniform and controlled application. Dry sprayed concrete, on the other hand, involves spraying dry materials which are then mixed with water at the nozzle. Each method has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs of the project.</p></p>
