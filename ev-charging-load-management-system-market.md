<p><ul><li>Dynamic Management</li><li>Static Management</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dynamic management in the EV Charging Load Management System market refers to real-time adjustments made to charging schedules based on current grid conditions, user demand, and energy prices. This ensures optimal utilization of resources and minimizes strain on the grid during peak times. </p><p>On the other hand, static management involves predetermined schedules and settings for charging, which do not change based on real-time factors. While static management is simpler and more cost-effective, dynamic management offers greater flexibility and efficiency in balancing charging needs with grid constraints.</p></p>
