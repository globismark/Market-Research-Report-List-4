<p><p>Galvanized iron wire is divided into two main types in the market: hot galvanized iron wire and cold galvanized iron wire. Hot galvanized iron wire is processed through high temperature to achieve corrosion resistance, making it suitable for outdoor use. On the other hand, cold galvanized iron wire is coated with a layer of zinc using electrolysis, providing similar protection but with a smoother finish. Both types of galvanized iron wire are widely used in construction, agriculture, and other industries for their durability and rust resistance.</p></p>
