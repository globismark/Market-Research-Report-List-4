<p><p>Business Ethernet Service market includes different types such as FTTC (Fiber To The Cabinet), FTTP (Fiber To The Premises), and EFM (Ethernet in the First Mile). FTTC refers to a fiber optic connection from the service provider to a distribution point near the customer's premises. FTTP is a direct fiber optic connection to the customer's premises. EFM utilizes multiple copper pairs to establish a high-speed Ethernet connection. Each market type offers varying levels of speed, reliability, and cost to businesses based on their specific needs and infrastructure requirements.</p></p>
