<p><p>MEMS pressure sensors are available in various types including capacitive pressure sensors, piezoresistive pressure sensors, and piezoelectric pressure sensors. </p><p>Capacitive pressure sensors measure pressure by detecting changes in capacitance. Piezoresistive pressure sensors use the piezoresistive effect to measure pressure by changes in resistance. Piezoelectric pressure sensors generate an electric charge in response to pressure, allowing for precise measurement. Each type of sensor has its own unique advantages and applications, making them suitable for a wide range of industries and technologies.</p></p>
