<p><ul><li>Flat Printer</li><li>Roller Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engraver printers are commonly classified into two main types: flat printers and roller printers. Flat printers have a flat surface where the material is placed for printing, making them ideal for printing on rigid materials like paper or wood. Roller printers, on the other hand, use a cylindrical roller to transfer the image onto the material, making them suitable for printing on curved or uneven surfaces. Each type of printer offers unique advantages depending on the material being used and the desired printing result.</p></p>
