<p><ul><li>Static Surface Tension Meter</li><li>Dynamic Surface Tension Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface tension meters are categorized into two main types: static surface tension meters and dynamic surface tension meters. Static surface tension meters measure the force required to separate a liquid-air interface in a stationary condition, providing information about the surface tension of the liquid. On the other hand, dynamic surface tension meters measure the change in surface tension over time, allowing for the analysis of dynamic changes in the liquid's behavior. Both types of surface tension meters are widely used in various industries for quality control and research purposes.</p></p>
