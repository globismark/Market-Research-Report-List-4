<p><p>DC power connectors are available in three main market types: surface mount, panel mount, and through hole. Surface mount connectors are soldered onto the surface of a PCB, making them suitable for compact designs. Panel mount connectors are mounted onto the surface of a panel or enclosure, providing easy access for users. Through hole connectors are soldered into holes on a PCB, providing a strong mechanical connection. Each type offers unique advantages depending on the application's requirements.</p></p>
