<p><p>Maize degerminators are used to remove the germ from maize kernels. There are two main types of maize degerminators - horizontal type and vertical type. Horizontal degerminators are designed with a horizontal rotor that rotates to separate the germ from the maize kernels. Vertical degerminators, on the other hand, have a vertical rotor that performs the same function. Both types of degerminators are commonly used in maize processing plants to improve the quality and yield of maize products.</p></p>
