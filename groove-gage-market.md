<p><p>Groove gages are available in three main market types: Digital Display, Pointer Display, and Caliper Reading. Digital Display groove gages show the measurement digitally, making it easier to read and record data quickly. Pointer Display gages use a pointer to indicate the measurement, giving a visual representation of the groove size. Caliper Reading gages require the use of a separate caliper to read and interpret the measurement. Each market type caters to different preferences and needs in terms of accuracy, ease of use, and data recording capabilities.</p></p>
