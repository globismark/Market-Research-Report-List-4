<p><p>Automotive Electric Window Regulator market types include Scissor Regulator, Cable Regulator, Flexible Shaft Regulator, and Others. Scissor Regulators consist of a system of connected bars that raise and lower the window. Cable Regulators use cables and pulleys to control the window movement. Flexible Shaft Regulators utilize a flexible shaft to transfer motion to the window regulator. Other types may include rack and pinion systems or hydraulic regulators. Each type offers unique features and benefits for controlling automotive window movement.</p></p>
