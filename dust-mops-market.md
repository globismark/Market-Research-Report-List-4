<p><p>Dust mops come in two main types: Ordinary Dust Mops and Electrostatic Dust Mops. Ordinary dust mops are designed to pick up dust and debris through their fibers and can be used on a variety of surfaces. On the other hand, electrostatic dust mops use static electricity to attract and trap dust particles, making them more effective at capturing fine dust and pet hair. Both types of dust mops are popular choices for household cleaning and maintenance.</p></p>
