<p><strong>The Pectin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin</li><li>Amidated low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin is commonly used in products that require a firm texture and high sugar content, such as jams and jellies. Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin is used in products that require a low sugar content and a softer texture, such as dairy products and beverages. Amidated low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin is a modified form of LMC Pectin that provides better stability in acidic conditions, making it suitable for applications like fruit fillings and acidified dairy products.</p></p>
