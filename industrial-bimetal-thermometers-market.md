<p><p>Industrial Bimetal Thermometers are commonly classified into two main types: Helix Strip Bimetal Thermometers and Spiral Strip Bimetal Thermometers. Helix Strip Bimetal Thermometers have a coil-like design, with the bimetallic strip wound into a helix shape. This design allows for accurate temperature measurements in a compact instrument. On the other hand, Spiral Strip Bimetal Thermometers have a spiral-shaped bimetallic strip, providing similar accuracy in temperature measurement but with a different visual appearance. Both types are commonly used in industrial applications for monitoring and controlling temperature.</p></p>
