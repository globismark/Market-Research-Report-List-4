<p><p>Bio-fuel Cell Market includes two main types: Microbial Fuel Cell (MFC) and Enzymatic Biofuel Cell. MFCs use microorganisms to convert organic matter into electricity, making them suitable for wastewater treatment and energy production. Enzymatic Biofuel Cells, on the other hand, utilize enzymes as catalysts to generate electricity by breaking down specific substrates. Both technologies have shown promise in sustainable energy production and have potential applications in various sectors such as healthcare, environmental monitoring, and portable electronics.</p></p>
