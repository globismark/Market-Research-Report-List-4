<p><ul><li>Potentiometric Sensors</li><li>Amperometric Sensors</li><li>Conductometric Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electrochemical sensor market is segmented into three main types: Potentiometric Sensors, Amperometric Sensors, and Conductometric Sensors. Potentiometric sensors measure electrical potential difference between electrodes and are widely used in pH sensors. Amperometric sensors detect current flow resulting from a redox reaction, commonly used in glucose meters. Conductometric sensors measure changes in electrical conductivity due to chemical reactions, often used in gas sensors. Each type of sensor offers unique advantages and applications in various industries such as healthcare, environmental monitoring, and industrial processes.</p></p>
