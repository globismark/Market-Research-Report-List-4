<p><p>Optical Fiber Distribution Box Market Types include the 1:4, 1:8, 1:16, and 1:32 Optical Splitters. The 1:4 Optical Splitter divides a single input signal into four outputs, while the 1:8 Optical Splitter separates it into eight outputs. Similarly, the 1:16 Optical Splitter splits the signal into sixteen outputs, and the 1:32 Optical Splitter divides it into thirty-two outputs. These different types cater to varying network requirements, offering flexibility and scalability for optical fiber distribution systems.</p></p>
