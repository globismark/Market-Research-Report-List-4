<p><ul><li>Blind Hole Method</li><li>Ring Core Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stress gauge market offers two main types of methods for measuring stress in materials: Blind Hole Method and Ring Core Method. The Blind Hole Method involves drilling a hole into the material and measuring the stress around the hole. On the other hand, the Ring Core Method uses a ring-shaped core that is inserted into the material to measure stress. Both methods provide accurate and reliable data for evaluating the stress levels in various materials, making them essential tools for industries such as manufacturing and construction.</p></p>
