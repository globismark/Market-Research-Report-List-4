<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Tapered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber Optic Plates (FOP) come in two main types: Straight and Tapered. Straight FOPs have a uniform thickness throughout the plate, ensuring consistent light transmission. They are commonly used in applications requiring a high level of resolution and uniformity, such as medical imaging and scientific research. On the other hand, Tapered FOPs have varying thickness, allowing for better collection efficiency and reduced light scattering. They are suitable for applications like astrophotography and night vision devices. Both types offer unique benefits depending on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
