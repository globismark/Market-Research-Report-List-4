<p><p>Starch derivatives market includes various types such as maltodextrin, cyclodextrin, glucose syrup, spray dried starch, and hydrolysates. Maltodextrin is commonly used as a thickening agent in food and pharmaceutical products. Cyclodextrin is used for encapsulating food ingredients or as a drug delivery system. Glucose syrup is a sweetening agent and thickening agent in food products. Spray dried starch is used in instant food products for quick rehydration. Hydrolysates are enzymes used to break down starch into simpler sugars for various industrial applications.</p></p>
