<p><p>Immersible pumps are categorized into three main types based on their applications: Non-clog submersible pumps are designed to handle wastewater and sewage without clogging. Openwell submersible pumps are used for pumping water from open wells or reservoirs. Borewell submersible pumps are specifically designed for extracting water from deep borewells. Each type of pump is suited for different purposes and environments, offering efficient and reliable water pumping solutions for various industrial, commercial, and residential applications.</p></p>
