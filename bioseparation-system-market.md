<p><p>The bioseparation system market encompasses various types of technologies for separating and purifying biological molecules. Chromatography technique involves separating components based on their different affinities for a stationary phase. Membrane-based bioseparation uses membranes to selectively separate molecules based on size or charge. Centrifugation technology separates components based on their density differences. Cell disruption technology breaks open cells to release their contents. Precipitation, extraction technique and filtration technologies are used to separate and isolate biomolecules based on their solubility, affinity or size.</p></p>
