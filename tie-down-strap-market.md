<p><p>Tie-down straps are commonly categorized into three main types based on their mechanisms: Cam Straps, Over-Center Lever Cam Style Straps, and Ratchet Straps. Cam Straps feature a cam buckle for easy tensioning, Over-Center Lever Cam Style Straps have a lever mechanism to secure the strap in place, and Ratchet Straps utilize a ratcheting system for precise tightening. Each type offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications, providing users with a variety of options to secure loads safely and efficiently.</p></p>
