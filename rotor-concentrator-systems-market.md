<p><p>Rotor Concentrator Systems are air pollution control devices used to treat volatile organic compounds (VOCs) emitted from industrial processes. These systems utilize a rotating wheel to concentrate the VOC-laden air stream, which is then directed to a thermal oxidizer for destruction. Rotor Concentrator Systems are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, chemical processing, and printing to comply with environmental regulations and reduce emissions.</p><p>The Rotor Concentrator Systems Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period. The increasing stringency of environmental regulations around the world is driving the demand for Rotor Concentrator Systems. Additionally, growing awareness about the harmful effects of VOC emissions on human health and the environment is also contributing to market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the Rotor Concentrator Systems Market is the integration of IoT technology for real-time monitoring and control of system performance.
