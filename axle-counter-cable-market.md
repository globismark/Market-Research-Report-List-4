<p><p>Axle Counter Cable market includes different types of cables such as Twisted Pair Cables and Star-quad Cables. Twisted Pair Cables are composed of two insulated copper wires twisted together to reduce electromagnetic interference. They are commonly used in data communication applications. On the other hand, Star-quad Cables consist of four insulated twisted pairs arranged in a star configuration to further enhance noise rejection. They are often used in high-interference environments to ensure accurate signal transmission. Both types of cables cater to specific needs in the market based on their unique characteristics.</p></p>
