<p><p>Dry heat sterilization involves the use of high temperatures to kill microorganisms and sterilize equipment. Two main types of dry heat sterilization methods are flame sterilization and hot air sterilization. Flame sterilization involves using an open flame to sterilize metal tools by passing them through the flame. Hot air sterilization, on the other hand, uses hot air ovens to heat equipment to high temperatures for a specified amount of time to achieve sterilization. Both methods are used widely in healthcare facilities and laboratories.</p></p>
