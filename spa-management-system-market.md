<p><p>Spa management systems can be classified into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based systems store data on remote servers, allowing users to access information from anywhere with an internet connection. On the other hand, on-premises systems store data locally within the spa, requiring physical infrastructure and maintenance. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, with cloud-based systems offering scalability and flexibility, while on-premises systems may provide greater control and security over data.</p></p>
