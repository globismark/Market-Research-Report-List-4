<p><p>Empty can inspection systems are classified into various market types based on the technology used for inspection. Optical inspection systems use cameras and image processing software to detect defects on the surface of cans. Ultrasonic testing systems use sound waves to identify defects inside the cans. X-ray inspection systems use radiation to penetrate the cans and detect any internal defects. Other market types may include systems using sensors or electromagnetic induction for inspection purposes. Each technology brings its own benefits and capabilities to ensure the quality of empty cans.</p></p>
