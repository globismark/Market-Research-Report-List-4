<p><p>Industrial overrunning clutches, such as sprag clutches and spring clutches, are widely used in various applications including heavy machinery and equipment. Sprag clutches utilize a series of ramps and rollers to engage and disengage the clutch, providing reliable and efficient torque transfer. On the other hand, spring clutches use a set of springs to engage the clutch and absorb shock loads. Both types of clutches offer benefits such as increased productivity, reduced maintenance costs, and improved operational safety in industrial settings.</p></p>
