<p><p>The Living Insecticide Market consists of three main types - Microbial Pesticides, Plant Pesticides, and Biochemical Pesticides. Microbial Pesticides are derived from microorganisms like bacteria and fungi to control pests. Plant Pesticides are made from natural plant extracts and compounds that repel or kill insects. Biochemical Pesticides are bio-based substances that disrupt the metabolism or behavior of pests. Each type offers a natural and eco-friendly alternative to traditional chemical insecticides, making them increasingly popular in the agricultural industry.</p></p>
