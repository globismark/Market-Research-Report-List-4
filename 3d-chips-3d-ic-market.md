<p><p>3D IC market includes various types such as 3D Wafer-Level Chip-Scale Packaging (WLCSP), 3D TSV, and others. 3D Wafer-Level Chip-Scale Packaging (WLCSP) involves stacking multiple semiconductor device dies on top of each other at the wafer level. 3D TSV (through-silicon via) technology involves vertically stacking multiple dies and connecting them with through-silicon vias. Other market types in the 3D IC market may include technologies such as interposer-based 3D stacking, die-to-wafer bonding, and monolithic 3D IC integration.</p></p>
