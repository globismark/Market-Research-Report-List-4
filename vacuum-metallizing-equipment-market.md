<p><strong>The Vacuum Metallizing Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Suspension Type</li><li>Roller Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vacuum metallizing equipment market includes suspension type and roller type systems. Suspension type equipment consists of a suspended target within the vacuum chamber, allowing for a more flexible coating process. Roller type equipment, on the other hand, uses a continuous roller system for coating materials. Both systems are commonly used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and electronics for applying metallic coatings on various substrates. The choice between suspension and roller type equipment depends on factors such as production volume, coating requirements, and budget considerations.</p></p>
