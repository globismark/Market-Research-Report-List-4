<p><p>Canned Motor Pumps are a type of centrifugal pump that incorporates the motor in the pump housing without the need for a mechanical seal. This design helps to prevent leakage of the pumped fluid, making canned motor pumps ideal for handling hazardous or environmentally sensitive chemicals.
