<p><ul><li>Resistive Muffler</li><li>Resistant Muffler</li><li>Impedance Composite Muffler</li><li>Micro Perforated Plate Muffler</li><li>Small Hole Muffler</li><li>Active Muffler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial duct exhaust mufflers come in various types to address different noise reduction needs. Resistive mufflers use sound-absorbing materials to reduce noise. Resonant mufflers use chambers and perforated tubes to cancel out noise. Impedance composite mufflers combine resistive and reactive elements for effective noise reduction. Micro perforated plate mufflers use fine perforations to dissipate sound energy. Small hole mufflers utilize small holes to attenuate noise. Active mufflers use technology to actively cancel out noise for maximum noise reduction in industrial settings.</p></p>
