<p><p>Hydraulic torque wrenches are commonly available in two types: drive hydraulic wrench and hollow hydraulic wrench. </p><p>1. Drive hydraulic wrenches are equipped with a square drive that directly fits onto the hex nut or bolt being tightened. They are versatile and can be used in various industries for a wide range of applications.</p><p>2. Hollow hydraulic wrenches have a hollow center that allows them to fit over long bolts or studs, making them suitable for applications where clearance is limited. They are commonly used in industries such as construction and mechanical engineering.</p></p>
