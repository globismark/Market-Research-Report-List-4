<p><p>Carpet backing materials are essential components of carpets, providing stability and structure. In the market, there are two main types of backing materials: primary backing, typically made of synthetic fabrics like polyester or nylon, and secondary backing, which can be either jute or woven polypropylene. Primary backing provides the base for the carpet fibers to be attached, while secondary backing adds further support and durability. Overall, these backing materials play a crucial role in determining the quality and lifespan of a carpet.</p></p>
