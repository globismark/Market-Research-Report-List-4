<p><strong>The Sliding Table Saw Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Driven Directly</li><li>Driven By Belt</li><li>Driven By Gears</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sliding table saws can be classified into three types based on their driving mechanisms: driven directly, driven by belt, and driven by gears. In directly driven saws, the motor is directly connected to the saw blade, providing strong and efficient power transfer. Belt-driven saws use belts to transfer power between the motor and the blade, offering quieter operation. Gears-driven saws utilize gears to transfer power, ensuring precise and controlled cutting. Each type has its unique advantages and may be preferred based on specific cutting requirements.</p></p>
