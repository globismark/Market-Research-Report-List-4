<p><p>The ETFE membrane market can be classified into three types based on thickness: below 200 µm, 200-300 µm, and above 300 µm. ETFE membranes below 200 µm are lightweight and ideal for applications requiring flexibility. Membranes in the 200-300 µm range offer a balance between flexibility and strength, making them suitable for various architectural projects. ETFE membranes above 300 µm are thicker and more durable, making them suitable for high-traffic areas or where additional strength is required. Each type caters to specific project requirements in the construction industry.</p></p>
