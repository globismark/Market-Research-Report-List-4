<p><p>Thermal insulation fabric market includes materials like glass fiber and mineral wool. Glass fiber insulation is made from molten glass that is spun into fibers, providing excellent thermal insulation properties. Mineral wool, on the other hand, is made from natural minerals like rock or slag that are melted, spun into fibers, and then compressed into insulation boards or rolls. Both types of insulation fabric offer high thermal resistance, fire resistance, and are widely used in construction and industrial applications for insulating buildings and equipment.</p></p>
