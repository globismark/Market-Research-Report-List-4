<p><p>Damper actuators are devices used in HVAC systems to control the opening and closing of dampers to regulate airflow. There are two main types of damper actuators: Non-spring return damper actuators and spring return damper actuators. Non-spring return damper actuators are known for their precise control and reliability, while spring return damper actuators are designed to return to a preset position if power is lost. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and effective HVAC system operation.</p></p>
