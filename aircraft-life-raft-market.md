<p><p>The Aircraft Life Raft market includes two main types: Single Tube Life Raft and Multi Tube Life Raft. Single Tube Life Rafts are designed as one continuous inflatable tube, providing a simple and compact solution for emergency situations. On the other hand, Multi Tube Life Rafts have multiple separate compartments or tubes, offering enhanced stability and redundancy in case of punctures or damage. Both types cater to the needs of different aircraft operators, depending on factors such as aircraft size, capacity, and safety requirements.</p></p>
