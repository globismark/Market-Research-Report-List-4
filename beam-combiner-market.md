<p><strong>The Beam Combiner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coherent Beam Combination</li><li>Spectral Beam Combination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coherent beam combination technology involves combining multiple laser beams to create a single, high-power output beam with high beam quality and brightness. This technology is commonly used in applications requiring high-power lasers, such as materials processing and defense systems. </p><p>Spectral beam combination technology, on the other hand, involves combining multiple laser beams of different wavelengths to achieve a high-power output beam covering a broad spectral range. This technology is used in applications such as medical treatments, spectroscopy, and telecommunications.</p></p>
