<p><p>Smart electricity meters come in two main types: Single Phase and Three Phase. Single Phase meters are typically used for residential properties, while Three Phase meters are commonly found in commercial and industrial settings. Single Phase meters have a lower voltage capacity and are suitable for smaller loads, while Three Phase meters can handle higher voltages and are designed for heavier electrical loads. Both types of meters offer advanced monitoring and communication capabilities, allowing for more efficient energy management and billing.</p></p>
