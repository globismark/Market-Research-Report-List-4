<p><strong>The Server Rail Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tapped/Threaded Rack Rail</li><li>Square Hole Rack Strip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Server rail kits come in two main types: Tapped/Threaded Rack Rail and Square Hole Rack Strip. Tapped/Threaded Rack Rail features pre-drilled holes with threading, making it easy to attach the rails to the server rack. On the other hand, Square Hole Rack Strip does not have pre-drilled holes but instead features square cutouts that allow for more flexibility in mounting different types of equipment. Both types of rail kits are essential for securely mounting servers and other equipment in a rack.</p></p>
