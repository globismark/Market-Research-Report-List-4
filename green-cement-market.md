<p><p>Green cement market types include fly-ash based, slag based, recycled aggregates, and others. Fly-ash based cement uses industrial by-products as a replacement for traditional cement materials, reducing carbon emissions. Slag based cement utilizes blast furnace slag to produce a more sustainable alternative to conventional cement. Recycled aggregates are made from crushed concrete and are used as a substitute for natural aggregates in cement production. Other types of green cement may include alternative materials and processes that aim to reduce environmental impact in the construction industry.</p></p>
