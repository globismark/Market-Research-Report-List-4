<p><p>Retroreflectors are optical devices that reflect incident light back in the direction it came from. There are different types of retroreflectors available in the market such as Single Mode Retroreflector, Polarization Maintaining Retroreflector, and Multimode Retroreflector. Single Mode Retroreflectors are designed to maintain the polarization state of light, while Polarization Maintaining Retroreflectors are used to maintain polarization in optical systems. Multimode Retroreflectors are used for multiple beam configurations and applications. Each type of retroreflector serves specific purposes in various optical systems.</p></p>
