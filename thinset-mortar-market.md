<p><ul><li>Dry-set Mortar</li><li>Latex-portland Cement Mortar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thinset mortar is a type of adhesive used to bond tiles to a substrate. Dry-set mortar is a type of thinset mortar that is mixed with water on-site before use, offering more control over consistency. Latex-portland cement mortar is a pre-mixed thinset mortar that contains added latex polymers for improved flexibility and water resistance. Both types of mortar are commonly used in tile installations, with dry-set mortar being preferred for its cost-effectiveness and versatility, while latex-portland cement mortar offers enhanced durability and adhesion properties.</p></p>
