<p><ul><li>Vitro Tissue</li><li>Internal Tissue</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Artificial tendons are categorized into two main types: in vitro tissue and internal tissue market. In vitro tissue refers to artificial tendons created outside the body using biomaterials in a lab setting. These tendons are then implanted into the patient's body during surgery. On the other hand, the internal tissue market involves the development of artificial tendons within the patient's body using stem cells or other regenerative medicine techniques. Both types aim to provide solutions for tendon injuries and improve overall patient outcomes.</p></p>
