<p><p>Control release fertilizers are classified into three main types: synthetic organic nitrogen fertilizer, coated fertilizer, and others. Synthetic organic nitrogen fertilizers are made from synthetic compounds that slowly release nitrogen to plants over time. Coated fertilizers have a special coating that controls the release of nutrients. The other types of control release fertilizers include blended fertilizers with various nutrient releases, such as slow-release formulas or water-soluble fertilizers. These different types cater to the specific needs of different crops and soil types.</p></p>
