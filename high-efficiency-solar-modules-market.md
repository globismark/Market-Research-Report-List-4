<p><p>High-efficiency solar modules market is dominated by two main types of technology - HJT Cells and IBC Cells. HJT (Heterojunction) Cells are known for their high conversion efficiency, achieved by reducing energy loss through improved electrical pathways. On the other hand, IBC (Interdigitated Back Contact) Cells utilize innovative designs to minimize shading and increase light absorption, resulting in higher overall efficiency. Both technologies are highly sought after in the solar industry for their superior performance and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
