<p><strong>The Next Generation Sequencers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sequencing by Synthesis (SBS)</li><li>Ion Semiconductor Sequencing</li><li>Single-Molecule Real-time (SMRT) Sequencing</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Next Generation Sequencers market involves various types of sequencing technologies. Sequencing by Synthesis (SBS) uses reversible terminator chemistry to identify nucleotides. Ion Semiconductor Sequencing relies on measuring changes in pH as nucleotides are incorporated into a growing DNA strand. Single-Molecule Real-time (SMRT) Sequencing observes the process of DNA synthesis in real time. Other markets include nanopore sequencing, which involves threading DNA through nanopores for sequencing, and spatial transcriptomics, which allows for gene expression analysis in a spatial context. Each technology offers unique advantages and applications in the field of genomics.</p></p>
