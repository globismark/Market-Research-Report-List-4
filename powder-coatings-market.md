<p><ul><li>Thermoset Powder Coating</li><li>Thermoplastic Powder Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder coatings come in two main types: Thermoset and Thermoplastic. Thermoset powder coating materials undergo a chemical reaction during the curing process, forming a durable and tough finish. On the other hand, Thermoplastic powder coatings do not undergo a chemical reaction during curing and can be melted and reformed. Both types offer excellent protection against corrosion, chemicals, and UV rays, making them popular choices for various industries such as automotive, appliances, and construction.</p></p>
