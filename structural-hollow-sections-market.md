<p><p>Structural Hollow Sections Market includes two main types: Hot Formed and Cold Formed. Hot Formed sections are made by heating steel and shaping it into desired forms. They are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. Cold Formed sections, on the other hand, are produced by bending flat steel sheets into shapes without heating. They offer cost-effective solutions for various construction projects and are commonly used in industries such as infrastructure, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
