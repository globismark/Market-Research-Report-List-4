<p><ul><li>Strip Guns</li><li>Fiber Guns</li><li>Slot Guns</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot melt glue guns are available in three main types - strip guns, fiber guns, and slot guns. Strip guns use glue in the form of solid sticks that are fed into the gun and melted as needed. Fiber guns use glue in the form of granules that are melted inside the gun before application. Slot guns are designed for precise application and use glue in the form of cartridges that are loaded into the gun. Each type of gun caters to different applications and preferences in the market.</p></p>
