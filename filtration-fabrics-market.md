<p><p>Filtration fabrics market types include Monolayer Weave, Double Weave, Three-Layer Weave, and other variations. Monolayer Weave fabrics consist of a single layer of woven material, providing basic filtration. Double Weave fabrics have two layers for enhanced filtration efficiency and durability. Three-Layer Weave fabrics offer even higher filtration performance due to an additional layer of material. Other market types may include speciality fabrics with unique properties or combinations of different weave patterns for specific filtration needs. Each type offers varying levels of filtration effectiveness and durability.</p></p>
