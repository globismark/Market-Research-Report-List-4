<p><strong>The Fuel Flexible Boiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Circulating Fluidised Bed Combustion (CFBC) boiler</li><li>Atmospheric Fluidised Bed Combustion (AFBC)</li><li>Multi fuel firing boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel flexible boiler market includes Circulating Fluidised Bed Combustion (CFBC) boilers, Atmospheric Fluidised Bed Combustion (AFBC) boilers, and Multi fuel firing boilers. CFBC boilers use a circulating bed of sand or limestone mixed with fuel, providing better efficiency and reduced emissions. AFBC boilers rely on a bed of ash and limestone for combustion, offering good fuel flexibility and lower emissions. Multi fuel firing boilers can use a variety of fuels such as coal, biomass, and waste, providing versatility and operational stability.</p></p>
