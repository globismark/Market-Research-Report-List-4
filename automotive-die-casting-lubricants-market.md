<p><p>Automotive die casting lubricants are used in the manufacturing process to facilitate the casting of metal components with minimal friction and heat generation. There are three main types of die casting lubricants: die lubricants, plunger lubricants, and others. Die lubricants are applied onto the die cavity to prevent sticking, while plunger lubricants are used on the plunger to facilitate smooth movement. Other types of lubricants may be used for specific applications or materials. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring efficient and high-quality die casting production.</p></p>
