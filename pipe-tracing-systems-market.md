<p><strong>The Pipe Tracing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Tracking</li><li>Electric Tracking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pipe tracing systems are used to maintain the temperature of pipes in various industries. Steam tracing involves wrapping pipes with heat conductive material and circulating steam to maintain temperature. Electric tracing uses electrical cables to generate heat along the pipe. Both systems are crucial in preventing freezing and improving the flow of liquids in pipes. The steam tracing market is driven by its cost-effectiveness and efficiency, while the electric tracing market is gaining popularity for its ease of installation and control.</p></p>
