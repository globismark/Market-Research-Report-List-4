<p><p>Retroreflective tapes are available in two main types: Glass Bead Type and Micro Prismatic Type. Glass Bead Type tapes are made with tiny glass beads that reflect light back to its source, making them ideal for low-light conditions. Micro Prismatic Type tapes have cube-corner microprisms that provide superior reflectivity and visibility, making them suitable for high-visibility applications. Both types of tapes are commonly used for enhancing visibility and safety in various industries such as construction, road signage, and transportation.</p></p>
