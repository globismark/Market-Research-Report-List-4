<p><p>Microscopy market consists of two main types: Optical Microscope and Electron Microscope. Optical microscopes use visible light to create images of tiny objects, suitable for viewing biological samples and materials like metals and ceramics. On the other hand, Electron microscopes use a beam of electrons to create a highly detailed image of samples, allowing for examination of structures at a much smaller scale. Both types have their own strengths and limitations, making them suitable for different applications in research, industry, and healthcare.</p></p>
