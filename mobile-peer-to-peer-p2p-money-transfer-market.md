<p><p>Mobile Peer-To-Peer (P2P) Money Transfer Market has two main types: Remote Payment and Proximity Payment. Remote Payment refers to transferring money between individuals or businesses using mobile devices over a network, regardless of their physical location. Proximity Payment, on the other hand, involves transferring money between individuals in close proximity, usually using technologies like near field communication (NFC). Both types offer convenient and efficient ways to send and receive money using mobile devices.</p></p>
