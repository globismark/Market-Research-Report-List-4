<p><p>Digital Business Support System market can be categorized into two main types: On-premise and Cloud-based. On-premise systems require hardware and software to be installed and managed locally on the company's premises. Cloud-based systems, on the other hand, are hosted and managed by a third-party provider over the internet. On-premise systems offer more control and customization, while cloud-based systems provide flexibility and scalability.
