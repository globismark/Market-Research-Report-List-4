<p><p>Metro Ethernet markets can be categorized into two types: OVC (Operator Virtual Connection) and EVC (Ethernet Virtual Connection). OVC refers to a market where operators provide Ethernet services by leasing network infrastructure from other providers. EVC, on the other hand, involves operators providing end-to-end Ethernet services using their own network infrastructure. Both OVC and EVC markets offer different opportunities for operators to enter and compete in the rapidly growing Metro Ethernet market, catering to the diverse needs of businesses and consumers.</p></p>
