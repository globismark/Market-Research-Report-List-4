<p><p>Differential pressure sensors are available in two main types: digital type and analog type. Digital differential pressure sensors provide digital output signals, making them more accurate and reliable for precise measurements. These sensors are preferred for applications that require high accuracy and automation. Analog differential pressure sensors provide electrical output signals that vary in relation to the pressure difference being measured. These sensors are commonly used in industrial applications where a continuous signal is required for monitoring and control purposes.</p></p>
