<p><ul><li>TEOM Monitor</li><li>Beta Attenuation Monitor</li><li>Other Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Particulate Matter Monitors can be categorized into different types, such as TEOM Monitor, Beta Attenuation Monitor, and Other Monitors. TEOM Monitor utilizes a balance to measure particulate matter concentration, while Beta Attenuation Monitor uses the absorption of beta radiation. Other Monitors include various technologies like optical particle counters, gravimetric filters, and light scattering sensors. Each type of monitor offers its own advantages and disadvantages in terms of accuracy, sensitivity, and cost, catering to different monitoring needs and requirements.</p></p>
