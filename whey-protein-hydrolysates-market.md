<p><p>The Whey Protein Hydrolysates market can be categorized into two types based on the method of hydrolysis: Enzymatic Hydrolysis and Natural Hydrolysis. Enzymatic Hydrolysis involves the use of specific enzymes to break down whey protein into smaller peptides, making it easier to digest and absorb. On the other hand, Natural Hydrolysis occurs through natural processes such as fermentation or aging. Both methods result in whey protein hydrolysates with improved solubility, digestibility, and bioavailability, making them popular among athletes and fitness enthusiasts.</p></p>
