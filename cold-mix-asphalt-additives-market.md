<p><ul><li>Emulsion-based Cold Mix Asphalt Additive</li><li>Solvent-based Cold Mix Asphalt Additive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emulsion-based Cold Mix Asphalt Additives are added to cold mix asphalt to improve adhesion, workability, and durability. They are water-based and environmentally friendly. Solvent-based Cold Mix Asphalt Additives, on the other hand, use solvent carriers to enhance mixing and coating properties. They are known for their quick drying times and ability to withstand harsh weather conditions. Both types of additives play a crucial role in enhancing the performance of cold mix asphalt in various construction applications.</p></p>
