<p><p>Styreneic Block Copolymers market types primarily include SEBS (styrene-ethylene-butylene-styrene), SIS (styrene-isoprene-styrene), and other variations. SEBS copolymers are known for their high elasticity and low compression set, making them ideal for applications requiring flexibility and durability. SIS copolymers are more commonly used in adhesive and sealant formulations due to their excellent tackiness and adhesion properties.
