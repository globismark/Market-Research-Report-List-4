<p><p>Braiding machinery market includes two main types - Vertical Braiders and Horizontal Braiders. Vertical braiders are machines where the carriers move vertically during the braiding process, resulting in a vertically braided product. On the other hand, horizontal braiders are machines where the carriers move horizontally during the braiding process, producing horizontally braided products. Both types of braiders are used in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and textile for manufacturing products like hoses, cables, and ropes.</p></p>
