<p><p>Peelable coatings are used for protecting surfaces and can be categorized into three main types based on the type of formulation - Solvent based, Water based, and Hot-melt. Solvent based peelable coatings are easy to apply and provide a durable barrier. Water based coatings are environmentally friendly and easy to remove. Hot-melt coatings offer high adhesion and are usually used for industrial applications. Each type of peelable coating caters to specific needs and requirements in various industries for surface protection.</p></p>
