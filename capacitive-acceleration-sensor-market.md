<p><p>Capacitive acceleration sensors are categorized based on the number of axes they measure: 1-axis, 2-axis, and 3-axis sensors. </p><p>1-axis sensors measure acceleration along a single direction, typically used in applications where movement is limited to one plane. </p><p>2-axis sensors measure acceleration along two orthogonal directions, suitable for applications where movement occurs in two dimensions. </p><p>3-axis sensors measure acceleration along three orthogonal directions, providing comprehensive data on movement in three-dimensional space. </p><p>Each type of sensor caters to specific application requirements for accurate and efficient acceleration measurement.</p></p>
