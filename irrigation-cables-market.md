<p><p>Irrigation cables come in two main types: single core and multi-core. Single core cables have a single conductor, making them simpler and more cost-effective for smaller irrigation systems. Multi-core cables, on the other hand, have multiple conductors bundled together, allowing for more complex and larger irrigation systems to be set up. Each type of cable has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific needs and requirements of the irrigation system in question.</p></p>
