<p><strong>The IV Fluid Warmer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Warming Method</li><li>Intravenous Warming Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IV Fluid Warmer market can be divided into two main types: Surface Warming Method and Intravenous Warming Method. Surface Warming Method involves warming the IV fluid externally before it enters the patient's body. Intravenous Warming Method, on the other hand, warms the IV fluid directly as it flows through a warming device attached to the IV line. Both methods are used to prevent hypothermia, maintain body temperature, and improve patient outcomes during intravenous fluid administration in healthcare settings.</p></p>
