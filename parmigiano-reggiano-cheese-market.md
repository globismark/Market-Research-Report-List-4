<p><p>Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese is typically divided into two main market types: natural and processed. Natural Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese is made from raw milk, aged for a minimum of 12 months, and contains no additives or preservatives. It is known for its sharp flavor and granular texture. Processed Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese, on the other hand, is made from pasteurized milk and may contain additives or preservatives to enhance shelf life or alter its texture. It is often less expensive and widely available in supermarkets.</p></p>
