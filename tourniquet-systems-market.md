<p><p>Tourniquet systems market includes two main types: tourniquet instruments and tourniquet cuffs. Tourniquet instruments are used to apply pressure and restrict blood flow in a targeted area, often used in surgical procedures. On the other hand, tourniquet cuffs are inflatable devices that are wrapped around a limb to control and monitor blood flow during surgeries or to prevent excess bleeding in emergency situations. Both types play a crucial role in medical settings for controlling bleeding and ensuring safe surgical procedures.</p></p>
