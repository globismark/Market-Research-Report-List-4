<p><ul><li>Power Amplifier</li><li>Low Noise Amplifier (LNA)</li><li>Signal-Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SATCOM amplifiers include power amplifiers, low noise amplifiers (LNAs), and signal amplifiers. Power amplifiers are used to increase the power of the input signal, ensuring that the signal can travel long distances without loss. LNAs reduce the noise in the signal, improving the overall signal-to-noise ratio. Signal amplifiers boost the strength of the signal to ensure it reaches its intended destination without degradation. Each type of amplifier plays a crucial role in enhancing the performance of SATCOM systems.</p></p>
