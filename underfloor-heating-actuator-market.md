<p><p>Underfloor Heating Actuators are devices used to control the flow of hot water in underfloor heating systems. There are two main types of actuators commonly used in these systems - linear actuators and rotary actuators. </p><p>Linear actuators move in a straight line, allowing for precise control over the flow of water through the heating system. Rotary actuators, on the other hand, rotate to control the flow. Both types of actuators play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and effective heating in underfloor systems.</p></p>
