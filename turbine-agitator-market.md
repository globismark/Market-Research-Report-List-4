<p><strong>The Turbine Agitator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top-entry Agitator</li><li>Side-entry Agitator</li><li>Bottom-entry Agitator</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Turbine agitator market types include top-entry, side-entry, bottom-entry, and others. Top-entry agitators are mounted from the top of the tank and are commonly used for mixing applications. Side-entry agitators are installed on the side of the tank and are ideal for blending and solid suspension tasks. Bottom-entry agitators are mounted at the bottom of the tank and are suitable for high-viscosity liquids. Other types include portable agitators and magnetic agitators, offering versatility for various mixing requirements.</p></p>
