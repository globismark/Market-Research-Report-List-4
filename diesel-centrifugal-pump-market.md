<p><p>Diesel centrifugal pump market includes various types such as axial flow pumps, mixed/radial flow pumps, peripheral pumps, and jet pumps. Axial flow pumps are designed for high flow rates at low pressures, while mixed/radial flow pumps combine aspects of both axial and radial flow. Peripheral pumps are used for moderate flow rates and high-pressure applications, while jet pumps are designed to create suction for drawing fluids. Each type caters to specific needs in industries such as agriculture, construction, and water treatment.</p></p>
