<p><p>The fully automated nucleic acid extractor market includes two main types: impeticbead type and straw type. Impeticbead type extractors utilize magnetic beads coated with specific nucleic acid-binding agents to isolate and purify DNA/RNA from samples. On the other hand, straw type extractors use a column-based method for nucleic acid extraction. Both types offer high efficiency and accuracy in nucleic acid extraction, making them popular choices in various research and diagnostic applications.</p></p>
