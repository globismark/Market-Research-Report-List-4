<p><p>Acid mordant dyes are categorized into 1:1 type metal complex dye and 1:2 type metal complex dye markets. 1:1 type metal complex dyes form a one-to-one bond with the metal ion present in the mordant, resulting in a strong and durable dyeing effect. On the other hand, 1:2 type metal complex dyes bond with the metal ion in a one-to-two ratio, offering a different set of properties and benefits in dyeing processes. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the acid mordant dye market.</p></p>
