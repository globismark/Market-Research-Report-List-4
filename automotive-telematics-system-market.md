<p><p>Automotive telematics systems can be classified into three main types: embedded, tethered, and integrated. Embedded systems are built into the vehicle and do not require external devices. Tethered systems rely on a smartphone or other external device for connectivity. Integrated systems combine both embedded and tethered capabilities for a seamless user experience. Each type offers unique features and benefits, catering to different preferences and requirements of drivers and manufacturers in the automotive telematics market.</p></p>
