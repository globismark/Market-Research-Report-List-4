There are several types of lightning isolators available in the market, including rod isolators, sphere isolators, horn isolators, and others. Rod isolators are tall vertical rods typically installed on the top of buildings. Sphere isolators use a spherical shape to attract lightning strikes. Horn isolators have a horn-shaped structure to direct electricity away from the building. Other types of isolators may offer unique designs for lightning protection.</p></p>
