<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Chillers</li><li>Scroll Chillers</li><li>Screw Chillers</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial chillers are categorized into various types based on their cooling mechanisms. Centrifugal chillers use a rotating impeller to create cool air. Scroll chillers use a scroll compressor to cool the air. Screw chillers use a screw compressor for cooling. Other market types in the commercial chiller industry include absorption chillers, which use heat to create a cooling effect. Each type of chiller has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on factors such as efficiency, cooling capacity, and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
