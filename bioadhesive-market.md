<p><p>Bioadhesives are classified into two main types: plant-based bioadhesives and animal-based bioadhesives. Plant-based bioadhesives are derived from natural sources such as soy, starch, and cellulose, making them eco-friendly and sustainable. On the other hand, animal-based bioadhesives are derived from proteins found in animals like collagen and gelatin. Both types of bioadhesives are widely used in various industries including healthcare, construction, and packaging due to their strong adhesive properties and biodegradable nature.</p></p>
