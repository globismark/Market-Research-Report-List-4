<p><p>Industrial forming press market is divided into three main types based on the level of automation: fully-automatic, semi-automatic, and manual. Fully-automatic presses require little to no human intervention during the forming process, offering high efficiency and precision. Semi-automatic presses involve some degree of human interaction, requiring operators to assist in certain tasks. Manual presses rely entirely on human operation, offering more control but lower efficiency. Each type caters to different industrial needs, with fully-automatic presses being ideal for high-volume production, while manual presses are suitable for smaller scale operations.</p></p>
