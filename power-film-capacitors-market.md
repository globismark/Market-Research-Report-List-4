<p><p>Power film capacitors are classified into various types based on the material used for the film. PPS film capacitors, made with polyphenylene sulfide film, offer high insulation resistance and thermal stability. PET film capacitors, using polyethylene terephthalate film, provide good electrical properties and reliability. PEN film capacitors, made with polyethylene naphthalate film, offer high capacitance stability and low dielectric absorption. Other types of film capacitors may include those made with materials like polycarbonate or polypropylene. Each type offers unique characteristics suited for different applications in the power industry.</p></p>
