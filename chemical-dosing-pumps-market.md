<p><p>Chemical dosing pumps market is segmented into three main types. Digital diaphragm pumps are designed for accurate and precise dosing of chemicals, using digital control technology. Diaphragm dosing pumps operate by flexing a diaphragm to displace the chemical. Oscillating positive displacement pumps use a rotating mechanism to displace and dose chemicals. Each type of pump offers specific advantages and is used in various industries for controlled chemical dosing applications.</p></p>
