<p><p>Nano Electromechanical Systems, or NEMS, are a type of technology that involves the integration of mechanical and electrical components at the nanoscale. The different types of NEMS markets include nanotubes, nanowires, nanofilms, nanobelts, and others. Nanotubes are hollow cylinders made of carbon atoms, nanowires are thin wires usually made of metals or semiconductors, nanofilms are thin layers of material, and nanobelts are elongated nanostructures. Each of these market types offer unique properties and applications in various industries.</p></p>
