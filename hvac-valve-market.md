<p><p>HVAC valve market includes various types such as Ball Valve, Globe Valve, Butterfly Valve, and Others. Ball valves are characterized by a spherical closure which allows for quick shutoff. Globe valves have a linear motion disk for regulating flow. Butterfly valves use a disc that rotates to control flow. Other types of HVAC valves may include gate valves, check valves, and pressure relief valves. Each type of valve offers unique advantages in controlling and regulating the flow of fluids in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems.</p></p>
