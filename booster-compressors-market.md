<p><ul><li>Single Stage Booster Compressors</li><li>Double Stage Booster Compressors</li><li>Multi Stage Booster Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Booster compressors are used to increase the pressure of gases between different stages of a compression process. Single stage booster compressors have one compression stage, double stage booster compressors have two stages for higher pressure ratios, and multi-stage booster compressors have three or more stages for even higher pressure ratios. Each type of booster compressor is designed to meet specific pressure requirements for various industrial applications, providing flexibility and efficiency in gas compression processes.</p></p>
