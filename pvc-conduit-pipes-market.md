<p><p>PVC conduit pipes are widely available in two main types in the market - flexible type and rigid type. Flexible PVC conduit pipes are designed to bend easily, making them suitable for installations in tight or curved spaces. On the other hand, rigid PVC conduit pipes are more durable and provide better protection for wires in straight runs. The choice between flexible and rigid types depends on the specific requirements of the installation, such as the layout of the space and the level of protection needed for the wires.</p></p>
