Methylcellulose is a synthetic fiber that promotes regularity and bowel health. Psyllium is a natural fiber that aids in weight management and cholesterol reduction. Wheat dextrin is a soluble fiber that supports gut health and blood sugar control. Each type of fiber supplement powder offers unique benefits for overall health and well-being.</p></p>
