<p><p>Emergency escape lighting systems are crucial for providing illumination in the event of a power failure or emergency situation. Two common types of emergency lighting systems are central emergency lighting and self-contained emergency lighting. </p><p>Central emergency lighting systems are connected to a centralized power source and provide lighting to a network of luminaires throughout a building. On the other hand, self-contained emergency lighting systems have individual battery-operated luminaires that provide localized lighting in specific areas. Both systems play a vital role in ensuring the safety and security of occupants during emergencies.</p></p>
