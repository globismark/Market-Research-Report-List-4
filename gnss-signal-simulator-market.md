<p><ul><li>Single Channel Simulator</li><li>Multi Channel Simulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Channel GNSS Signal Simulators are designed to simulate the signals of a single satellite at a time, allowing for precise testing and analysis of individual satellites. On the other hand, Multi-Channel GNSS Signal Simulators can simulate multiple satellites simultaneously, providing a more comprehensive and realistic testing environment for GNSS receivers. Single Channel Simulators are typically more cost-effective for basic testing needs, while Multi-Channel Simulators offer more advanced functionalities for complex scenarios. Both types play a crucial role in the development and testing of GNSS technology.</p></p>
