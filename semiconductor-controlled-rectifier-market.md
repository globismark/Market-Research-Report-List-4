<p><p>Semiconductor Controlled Rectifier (SCR) Market comprises two main types: Unidirectional SCR Modules and Bidirectional SCR Modules. Unidirectional SCR Modules are designed to conduct current in one direction only, making them suitable for applications requiring unidirectional power flow. On the other hand, Bidirectional SCR Modules can conduct current in both directions, offering versatility in applications where power needs to flow bidirectionally. Both types play a crucial role in controlling and rectifying the flow of electrical current in a variety of industrial and consumer electronic devices.</p></p>
