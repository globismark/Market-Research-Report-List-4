<p><p>Dry Cooling Tower market can be divided into two main types: Open Cooling Tower and Closed Cooling Tower. Open Cooling Tower is a type of tower where water is allowed to come in direct contact with the air to cool it down, while Closed Cooling Tower involves the use of a heat exchanger to cool the water without direct contact with the air. Both types of cooling towers are used in various industries for cooling purposes, with Open Cooling Towers being more common in power plants and Closed Cooling Towers being more prevalent in chemical processing plants.</p></p>
