<p><p>Plastic pigment market types include inorganic pigment and organic pigment. Inorganic pigment, made from minerals, offers a wide range of colors and high durability. It is commonly used in applications requiring heat resistance and lightfastness. On the other hand, organic pigment, derived from carbon-based molecules, provides vibrant colors and excellent dispersibility. It is preferred for its eco-friendly properties and compatibility with various plastics. Both types of pigments play a crucial role in enhancing the visual appeal and functionality of plastic products.</p></p>
