<p><p>Cyclic Olefin Copolymer (COC) is a type of polymer that combines the characteristics of cyclic olefins and copolymers, offering high transparency, low water absorption, and excellent chemical resistance. Both COC and COP are widely used in packaging, optical, and medical industries due to their unique properties, including high clarity, low birefringence, and good moldability.
