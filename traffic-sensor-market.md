<p><p>Traffic sensors are crucial for monitoring and managing traffic flow. Inductive loop sensors detect vehicles through changes in inductance, while piezoelectric sensors use pressure to detect vehicles. Bending plate sensors detect weight changes on the road surface. Image sensors capture images for traffic analysis, and infrared sensors detect vehicles through heat signatures. Other sensors include ultrasonic and microwave sensors. Each type of sensor plays a unique role in collecting data to optimize traffic management strategies.</p></p>
