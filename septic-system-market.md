<p><strong>The Septic System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Gravity Septic System</li><li>Conventional-Pump Septic System</li><li>Pressure Manifold Septic System</li><li>Low-Pressure Pipe (LPP) Septic System</li><li>Drip Disposal Septic Systems</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The septic system market can be categorized into different types based on their design and function. Conventional gravity septic systems rely on the force of gravity to move wastewater through the system. Conventional-pump septic systems use pumps to facilitate the movement of wastewater. Pressure manifold septic systems distribute wastewater evenly across the drain field. Low-pressure pipe (LPP) septic systems use low-pressure pipes to distribute wastewater. Drip disposal septic systems involve the slow, controlled release of wastewater into the soil. There are also other types of septic systems available in the market.</p></p>
