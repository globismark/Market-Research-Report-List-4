<p><p>Semiconductor backend visual inspection systems are divided into two main types: optical based and infrared based. Optical based systems use visible light to inspect for defects or anomalies in semiconductor components, while infrared based systems use infrared light to detect heat variations or other irregularities. Both types of systems provide crucial quality control measures in the semiconductor industry, ensuring that products meet the necessary standards before being integrated into electronic devices. These systems play a vital role in preventing defects and malfunctions in the final products.</p></p>
