Straight-through carriers allow the exhaust gases to pass through the channels directly, while wall-flow carriers force the gases to flow through the porous walls, increasing contact with the catalyst and maximizing the conversion of harmful emissions into less harmful substances. Both types play a crucial role in reducing pollutants emitted from vehicles and improving air quality.</p></p>
