<p><p>Commercial aviation engine market consists of various types of engines that are used in aircraft. The most common types include Turbojet engines, which use exhaust gases to generate thrust. Turbofan engines use a combination of exhaust gases and bypass air to generate thrust, making them more fuel-efficient. Turboprop engines use a turbine to drive a propeller, which is ideal for smaller aircraft. Lastly, turboshaft engines are used in helicopters and other rotary-wing aircraft to power the rotor system. Each type of engine has its own unique advantages and applications in the aviation industry.</p></p>
