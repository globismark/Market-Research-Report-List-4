<p><p>Automatic faucets can be categorized into two main types based on their power source: DC and AC operated faucets. DC operated faucets are powered by direct current electrical supply, which is typically provided by batteries or a power adapter. On the other hand, AC operated faucets rely on alternating current electrical supply, commonly found in commercial buildings and residences. Each type has its own advantages and drawbacks, with DC faucets being more energy-efficient and easier to install, while AC faucets may provide a more reliable and continuous power source.</p></p>
