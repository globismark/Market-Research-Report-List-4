<p><p>Hot swap controllers are devices that allow electronic components to be inserted or removed from a system without disrupting its operation. There are different types of hot swap controllers based on the application requirements. High voltage hot swap controllers are designed for systems with higher operating voltages, while low voltage hot swap controllers are meant for lower voltage applications. PCI hot swap controllers specifically cater to the PCI bus architecture, allowing for the safe insertion and removal of PCI cards without causing damage to the system.</p></p>
