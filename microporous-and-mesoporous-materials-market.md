<p><ul><li>Microporous Materials (Less than 2 nm)</li><li>Mesoporous Materials (2-50 nm)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Microporous materials refer to materials with pores smaller than 2 nanometers in size, allowing for excellent selectivity and high surface area. These materials are widely used in applications such as gas separation and catalysis. On the other hand, mesoporous materials have pores ranging from 2 to 50 nanometers, offering a balance between fast mass transport and high surface area. They find applications in adsorption, drug delivery, and sensing. Both types of materials play a significant role in various industries due to their unique properties and functionalities.</p></p>
