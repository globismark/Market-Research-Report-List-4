<p><strong>The Portable Water Purifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RO</li><li>Ion Exchange</li><li>Filtration</li><li>Distillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable water purifiers come in various types, such as reverse osmosis (RO), ion exchange, filtration, and distillation. RO purifiers use a semi-permeable membrane to remove contaminants. Ion exchange purifiers work by exchanging ions to remove impurities. Filtration purifiers pass water through a filter to sieve out pollutants. Distillation purifiers boil water to steam and then condense it back to liquid form, removing contaminants in the process. Each type of purifier has its unique method of purifying water for safe consumption.</p></p>
