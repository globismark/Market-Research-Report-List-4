<p><ul><li>H264</li><li>H265</li><li>VP9</li><li>AV1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Video transcoding market types refer to different encoding formats used for compressing and decompressing video files. H264, also known as AVC, has been a widely adopted standard offering high compression efficiency. H265, or HEVC, provides even better compression rates at the cost of higher processing power. VP9 is an open-source alternative developed by Google, offering high-quality streaming for online videos. AV1 is the latest codec promising advanced compression technology and improved video quality while maintaining low bandwidth usage. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences in video transcoding technology.</p></p>
