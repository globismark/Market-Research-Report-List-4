<p><p>Noise Monitoring Stations Market consists of two main types: Portable Noise Monitoring Systems and Permanent Noise Monitoring Systems. Portable systems are easy to deploy and can be moved to different locations to measure noise levels. They are commonly used for short-term studies or in temporary work sites. Permanent systems are installed in a fixed location and continuously monitor noise levels over a long period of time. They are typically used in urban areas, industrial sites, or around transportation hubs to assess and manage noise pollution.</p></p>
