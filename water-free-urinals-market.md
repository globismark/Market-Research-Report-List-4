<p><ul><li>Liquid Sealant Cartridges</li><li>Membrane Traps</li><li>Biological Blocks</li><li>Mechanical Balls</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water free urinals are available in different market types such as liquid sealant cartridges, membrane traps, biological blocks, and mechanical balls. Liquid sealant cartridges use a liquid barrier to trap odors and prevent urine from entering the drain. Membrane traps have a flexible membrane that allows liquid to pass through but blocks odors. Biological blocks contain enzymes that break down urine and eliminate odors. Mechanical balls use a floating ball to create a barrier between urine and the drain. Each type offers a unique solution for water conservation and odor control in restrooms.</p></p>
