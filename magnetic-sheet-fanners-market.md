<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet</li><li>Electromagnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic sheet fanners are available in two main types: permanent magnet and electromagnetic. Permanent magnet sheet fanners use permanent magnets to separate and fan out sheets of material, providing a continuous fanning action without the need for an external power source. Electromagnetic sheet fanners, on the other hand, use an electric current to create a magnetic field that can be turned on and off as needed. Both types of sheet fanners are effective in industrial applications for separating and handling metal sheets.</p></p>
