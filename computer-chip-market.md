<p><p>The Computer Chip market can be classified into two main types: Southbridge and Northbridge. Southbridge chips are responsible for managing the slower capabilities of a computer system, such as connecting peripheral devices and handling input/output operations. On the other hand, Northbridge chips control the faster components of a computer system, such as the memory, graphics card, and processor. Both Southbridge and Northbridge chips play crucial roles in ensuring the smooth functioning and efficient performance of a computer system.</p></p>
